// About Us

The Story Behind LMA

LMA

Est. 2020

JAJPUR ROAD · ODISHA

Built on a belief that every student deserves excellence.

Learn Me Academy was founded in July 2020 by visionary educator Mr. Subrat Sahoo, with the dream of building an institution of excellence dedicated to the pursuit of perfection through holistic education.

Located in front of Rajmahal Gate No. 2, Dhabalgiri, Jajpur Road — LMA is a +2 Science Day-Cum Residential Institution offering CHSE & CBSE curriculum, NEET/JEE coaching, and CBSE Classes 8th-10th, with a 100% result in 2026.

"Sprouting hidden talents" isn't just our tagline — it's the foundation our faculty and methodology are built around.
